Getting Started

The concept is fairly simple. It takes in a word and fetches synonyms for that word. Then, it makes domain names out of the synonyms and checks to see if they are registered.

The domains are assembled like this:

prefix + synonym + suffix + tld

So, as an example:

If you were to enter guard as your word,
with a prefix of smart,
and a tld of .com,
one of the domains to be checked would be
smartprotect.com
